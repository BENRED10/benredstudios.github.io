About

A creative producer building across sound, visuals and interaction.

I’m a Creative Music Production student and the creative behind BENRED Studios, with a focus on sound, visual media and modern creative workflows. My work is built around creating strong moods, clean ideas and immersive experiences that can live across music, film, games and digital media.

My main background is in music production and sound design, but I’m also developing skills in graphic design, video editing, web design and game development. I’m interested in how these areas connect — for example, how sound can shape a visual, how design can support a project, or how editing can make a creative idea feel more professional.

Main focus Sound, visual media and audio-led creative work.
