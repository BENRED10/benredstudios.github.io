Featured Project

Survival Shooter FMOD Integration

For my CA1 project, I worked on a survival shooter game to practise FMOD and game sound design. I created the music and sounds myself, then integrated them into the game so the audio reacted to gameplay moments such as powerups, health changes, safe areas and rising danger levels.

The project helped me explore adaptive audio systems, including automation for gameplay feedback and changes in intensity depending on the player’s situation.

Graded Practical

Horror Platformer Sound Design

For another graded practical, I created the full sound design for a simple side-scrolling horror platformer that originally had no sound. I used FMOD to build and integrate the audio so the game felt more atmospheric, responsive and complete.

This project also gave me my first chance to experiment with sample variation for footsteps, using FMOD randomisation so repeated movement sounds felt more natural when triggered through Unity’s code.
