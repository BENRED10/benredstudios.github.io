Overview

My graphic design work focuses on creating visuals that feel bold, atmospheric and connected to the identity of a project. I often design around music, sound and creative media, using texture, contrast, typography and composition to create artwork that feels distinctive and professional.

Project Focus

This section includes cover artwork, promotional posters, typography-based visuals and branding experiments. Each piece explores how image treatment, layout and type can shape the mood of a project and communicate its identity clearly.

Featured Design

Will AI Make Music Producers Obsolete?

A promotional poster designed for a final year creative research project exploring the impact of artificial intelligence on music production. The design uses bold typography, a monochrome visual style and a structured poster layout to create a strong academic and professional identity.

Tools used: Affinity and Canva.

Selected Designs

A selection of design pieces exploring cover artwork, promotional visuals, typography and music-related branding. Each project uses layout, texture and visual mood to create a clear identity for the work.
