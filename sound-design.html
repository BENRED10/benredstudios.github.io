Featured Sound Redesign

Dead Space — Gameplay Sound Redesign

A sound redesign project where I replaced the original gameplay audio with custom-designed sound effects, ambience and layered audio elements.

The aim was to build tension, support the sci-fi horror atmosphere and demonstrate my approach to sound design for gameplay.

Animated Short Film

PÚCA — Animated Short Film Sound Design

For this animated short film, I collaborated with a college friend on the music and sound design for a project created by IADT animation students.

My main role focused on sound design, instrument selection, and the mixing and mastering of the music, while my collaborator composed the score using MIDI.

Sound Redesign Exercise

The Incredibles — Sound Redesign Exercise

A non-commercial portfolio exercise where I redesigned the sound for a scene from The Incredibles, using custom sound effects, recorded audio and layered sound design to support the action, timing and atmosphere of the scene.

Original film footage, music and source clip belong to their respective rights holders. This project is included only to demonstrate my sound design, editing and audio-to-picture skills.

Sound Design Approach

For this type of work, I focus on matching sound to movement, pacing and atmosphere. I build audio in layers so that impacts, ambience, transitions and detailed effects work together rather than feeling separate from the picture.
